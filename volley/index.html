Questions worth asking before you start.

How much time do I really need per day? +

Ten to fifteen minutes is enough to keep your plan on track. Most learners do a lesson on their commute, an AI conversation at lunch, and a few flashcards before bed. No textbook, no desk required.

Which languages can I learn? +

Spanish, French, Italian, German and Brazilian Portuguese today. Japanese and Mandarin open later this year — join the waitlist after your placement test.

Is the human coach actually human? +

Yes. Every Volley coach is a vetted language teacher with 5+ years of experience. They personally review your week, leave voice notes, and rebuild your next plan. The AI handles reps so they can focus on you.

What if I'm a complete beginner? +

Perfect — Volley is designed for adults starting from zero who don't want to spend two years in textbooks. The 2-minute placement test puts you on the right starting plan.

How does the 90-day guarantee work? +

Follow your personalized plan for 90 days. If you can't hold a real conversation at the end, we extend your coaching for another 90 days at no charge. Plan completion is tracked automatically — there's no form to fill out.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes — one click in your account. No phone calls, no retention pitch.
